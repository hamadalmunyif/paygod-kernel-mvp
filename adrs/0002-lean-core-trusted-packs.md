# ADR-0002: Lean Core, Trusted Packs Governance

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The project is open-source and needs low friction for contributors, while maintaining high assurance for trusted packs.

## Decision
- Adopt **Lean Core, Trusted Packs** model.
- Default packs are Community.
- Packs may be promoted to Verified with stricter criteria (reviews, pinning, signing).

## Consequences
- Community can contribute quickly.
- Verified packs provide enterprise-ready trust without imposing bureaucracy on the core.
